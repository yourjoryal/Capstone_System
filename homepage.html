    About

    The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program at CTU aims to produce highly skilled, innovative, and globally competitive IT professionals ready to meet industry demands.

    Vision

    To be a premier IT program recognized for producing competent, innovative, and ethical professionals who contribute to technological advancement.

    Mission

    The BSIT program aims to:
    ● Equip students with technical knowledge and practical skills.
    ● Foster creativity and problem-solving.
    ● Instill professional ethics and lifelong learning.
    ● Promote research, innovation, and collaboration.

    Goals

    ‣ Develop proficiency in programming, networking, and software development.
    ‣ Prepare graduates for successful IT careers through industry exposure.
    ‣ Encourage community involvement through IT solutions.
    ‣ Enhance communication, teamwork, and leadership skills.

    Outcome

    Graduates are expected to:
    1. Demonstrate competence in software and IT fields.
    2. Apply critical thinking to technological solutions.
    3. Exhibit professionalism and ethical behavior.
    4. Engage in lifelong learning and innovation.
    5. Collaborate effectively in diverse environments.
